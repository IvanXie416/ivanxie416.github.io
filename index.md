## About Me



I am currently a Ph.D. student in Data Science and Information Technology at [Tsinghua University](https://www.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/), under the supervision of Prof. [Wenbo Ding](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=xo2FkgIAAAAJ&hl=en). 
Previously, I obtained my Master's degree from [Xi'an Jiaotong University](https://en.xjtu.edu.cn/) in 2025, supervised by Prof. [Jihua Zhu](https://scholar.google.com.hk/citations?hl=zh-CN&user=JcN97sQAAAAJ).

## Research Interests

- **Embodied Intelligence**
- **Multi-Modal Learning** 
- **3D Vision**
